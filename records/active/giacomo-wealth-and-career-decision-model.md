## Core Objective

- Giacomo's primary life objective, as stated in this conversation, is to optimize revenue.
- The motivation is defensive as much as aspirational: he believes the global economy is unstable, inflation is rising, inequality will increase, and the gap between the poor and the rich will widen.
- His desired response is to move toward the wealthier side of that distribution and let compound interest work over time.
- He currently feels confused about the future path because many near-term gates are controlled by others: applications can be submitted, but outcomes depend on external confirmation.
- He described the fear of staying in place if no one confirms the next step.

## Capital Allocation Discipline

- Increase income.
- Do not increase spending.
- Invest the majority of surplus income.
- Current investing habit: about 500 euros per month, described as approximately 40% of salary.
- Current ETF allocation plan under investigation: 40% Europe, 40% United States, 20% Japan.
- The ETF split is intended to be simple while remaining geopolitically aware.
- Giacomo has started investing with this plan while still investigating whether the split is optimized.

## Income-Increase Paths Under Consideration

- Big tech: highest salary and strong privileges, but the application process is time-consuming and uncertainty seems high, especially with AI-driven layoffs. Requires automating CV uploads and preparing for technical interviews.
- Startups: fun, fast, bleeding-edge environments aligned with what Giacomo wants to experience. Risk is high, but the payoff could be larger and could include not working for someone else.
- Solopreneurship: high risk and high reward. Requires having the idea, knowing how to sell it, distributing it, and scaling it. Giacomo believes he knows roughly half of that. The appeal is control and creating something useful for someone. Marc Lou's story was especially motivating.

## Current Job Friction

- Giacomo does not currently exclude any of the income-increase paths.
- He feels wasted at his current job.
